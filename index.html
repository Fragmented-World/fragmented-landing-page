Fragmented

A decentralized social network
for Ethereum enthusiasts.


Everyone should have a voice.

It's time to build a platform for free speech that is owned by the people.

Read about the vision for Fragmented.

Monetized

Earn money from posting popular content. Tip authors that you support by upvoting and commenting. Get a share of the ad fees that the Fragmented generates, just by using the site.

Censorship Resistant

Fragmented will be a safe place to peacefully share your opinions without unjust censorship. Unlike Reddit, moderators will be elected, and if they abuse their power, you can vote them out.

Decentralized

Fragmented will eventually use IPFS or Swarm to host content. It will be one of the first organizations in the world to use Aragon or Colony for decentralized governance.
